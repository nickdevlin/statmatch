StatMatch

In this game, you have five turns to place each player in the slot that accurately reflects that player's career total. While some players could be placed in more than one slot, there is only one correct combination of all 16 players.

Once you have the players in the preferred order, hit the Check button. Players in the correct spot will show up with a green border, while those in the correct row but not the right spot will show up yellow.

Baseball players are featured in this prototype, but the game could be easily adapted to fit any sport, or even more multiple sports.

- Nick Devlin, February 2026
